I am a Research Associate at Brown University, working with Professors David Borton and Wilson Truccolo. I am engineering apparatus and performing primate PRT to understand volitional control of locomotion in non-human primates.

Previously, I was a research engineer at UC Berkeley with Professor Preeya Khanna, understanding dexterous manipulation in primates. I was a senior postgraduate student in Cognitive Science at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, where I worked on decoding brain states using machine learning and deep learning approaches from electroencephalography (EEG) signals. I have also worked on building a cable-driven upper-limb exoskeleton. I have a bachelor's in Computer Science and have worked as a full-stack software developer.

I will be joining Brown University's Biomedical Engineering Doctoral program starting Fall 2026.
